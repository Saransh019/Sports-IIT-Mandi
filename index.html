IIT Mandi has a sports council to coordinate all the sports activities and lays special attention on players by providing them with well-experienced coaches and adequate facilities. Sportsmen are trained to participate in various tournaments like Inter-IIT, Sangram, and other tournaments. IIT Mandi has participated in the Inter-IIT sports tournament since 2010 and has won medals in team games i.e. bronze in TT and in Cricket and 7 medals in individual Athletics events. It also organizes the annual sports fest of IIT Mandi- Rann-Neeti.

Events

Rann Neeti

Rann-Neeti is the Annual Inter College Sports Fest of IIT Mandi organised by Student Gymkhana of IIT Mandi. It is a three-day long event held every year in the month of September in which college from states all over the nation participate


AAGAZ

Aagaz is the annual inter year sports tournament held every year in the spring semester to encourage the spirit of sports among the students of IIT Mandi.


KPL

Kamand Premier League is an auction based event open for all the students and the faculty of the college.


Hostel GC

The Hostel General championship is an intra college event where the participants represent the hostel they live in.

Inter IIT

Inter IIT Sports Meet is the annual sports tournament of the Indian Institutes of Technology. It is organized in December. It is the longest running Inter-collegiate meet where all IITs participate having been held since 1961.
